Cancellation Policy

General Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancellations for ongoing services must be requested 7 days before the next billing cycle to avoid charges.
  • Cancellations after services have commenced will not be refunded for that month.

Project-Based Services:

  • Cancellations must be made within 48 hours to receive a full refund of any advance payments made.
  • After 48 hours, refunds will depend on the percentage of the project completed.

Digital Marketing Campaigns:

  • Cancellations for ad campaigns must be made 5 business days before the campaign starts.
  • Once a campaign has started, no cancellations or refunds for ad spend or management fees.
